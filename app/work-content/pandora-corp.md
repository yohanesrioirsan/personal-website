## Background

Pandora Corp is one of the most prominent gaming organizations in East Indonesia, with an active presence across multiple esports titles and a growing roster of competitive players. The founder and CEO, Herwin Long, reached out to me to build a company profile website that would serve as their official public-facing introduction a digital home that communicates who Pandora Corp is, what they do, and why they matter in the regional esports scene.

The brief was clear: the website needed to feel professional and credible, worthy of representing a serious organization. ## Solution

### Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Working closely with Pandora Corp's in-house graphic design team to bring the visual direction to life, I was also responsible for making sure the website didn't just look good it had to be found.

I implemented a thorough SEO setup so that search engines could properly crawl, understand, and index the site. This included:

- **Semantic HTML structure** Using proper heading hierarchy and meaningful markup so search engines can interpret the content accurately
- **Meta tags & Open Graph** Configuring title tags, meta descriptions, and OG tags for consistent appearance across search results and social media shares
- **Next.js metadata API** Leveraging Next.js's built-in metadata support to manage SEO configuration at both the page and layout level
- **Readable URL structure** Clean, descriptive routes for each section (players, shop, about) to improve both indexability and user experience
- **Performance optimization** Fast load times via Next.js's static generation and image optimization, since page speed is a direct factor in search ranking

The goal was to make sure that anyone searching for Pandora Corp whether a potential sponsor, a fan, or a journalist covering the East Indonesia esports scene would land on this site.

## Key Features

- **Company Profile** A polished introduction to Pandora Corp: their mission, their history, and their place in the East Indonesia gaming ecosystem
- **Players Page** Showcases both current active rosters and alumni, giving recognition to the players who have represented the organization
- **Shop Page** A dedicated section for Pandora Corp merchandise, extending their brand beyond the competitive stage
- **About Page** Deeper context on the organization's background, values, and the team behind it
- **Sponsor & Contact** A clear, accessible channel for sponsors and partners to reach out directly to the organization
